THE EVOLUTION OF DATA ANALYSIS TOOLS: FROM SPREADSHEETS TO ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AGENTS

Oleksii Tumanov

Oleksii Tumanov (2025). THE EVOLUTION OF DATA ANALYSIS TOOLS: FROM SPREADSHEETS TO ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AGENTS. Modern engineering and innovative technologies, 42-03, 110-120.

DOI: 10.30890/2567-5273.2025-42-03-055

Record Note

This review article traces the evolution of data analysis tools from spreadsheets and corporate BI platforms to cloud-native data stacks and agentic systems built on large language models. It examines the methodological shift from syntax-centered work to semantic layers, reproducibility, orchestration, and evaluation-by-design, and argues that the most promising direction is an integrated analytical environment where traditional statistical tools, modern data platforms, and AI agents complement rather than replace one another.
