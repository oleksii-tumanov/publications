The Evolution of Data Analysis Tools: From Spreadsheets to Artificial Intelligence Agents

Oleksii Tumanov

Tumanov, O. (2025). The Evolution of Data Analysis Tools: From Spreadsheets to Artificial Intelligence Agents. Modern engineering and innovative technologies, 110-120.

Public catalog page prepared from the local publication draft and public Google Scholar listing.

Abstract

This review article traces the evolution of data analysis tools from spreadsheets and corporate BI platforms to cloud-native data stacks and, finally, agentic systems built on large language models. It examines the methodological shift from syntax-centered work to semantic layers, reproducibility, orchestration, and evaluation-by-design, and argues that the most promising direction is an integrated analytical environment where traditional statistical tools, modern data platforms, and AI agents complement rather than replace one another.
