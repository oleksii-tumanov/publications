Article

Large Language Models: A New Paradigm for Data Analysis

Oleksii Tumanov

Oleksii Tumanov (2025). Large Language Models: A New Paradigm for Data Analysis. Business Inform, 9 (572), 106-113.

DOI: 10.32983/2222-4459-2025-9-106-113

Record Note

The article examines how large language models are changing data analysis across domains such as social media analytics, healthcare, and software development. It compares LLM-based approaches with traditional statistical methods across data type, interpretability, purpose, data requirements, and reproducibility, and argues that the strongest future direction is hybrid methodology that combines LLM preprocessing with classical statistical analysis for greater transparency, reliability, and accuracy.
